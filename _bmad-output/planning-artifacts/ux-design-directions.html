Direction E: Subtle Warmth ★ Recommended
Discord's proven layout with slightly softer edges (6px radius), subtly warmer hover states, and a touch more breathing room. The "if Discord was designed by someone who cared about comfort" direction. Familiar enough to trigger muscle memory, refined enough to feel better.
